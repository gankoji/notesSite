AME Rejection :(

After 2 full years in the AME PhD program at UofA, slogging my way through course after course and pushing myself to my limits mentally and sometimes even physically, I was informed rather sharply that my advisement situation would abruptly end at the end of last semester. Dr. Rosengren was leaving to join the faculty at USD, and Dr. Butcher simply wasn't interested in working with me anymore.

To be completely honest, I'm glad it worked out that way, because Butcher was more than a bit of a douche who could never be bothered to remember who the hell I was, let alone what I was working on. I was a bit bummed out by Aaron leaving, but hey that's life. The real sad part is that the other professor I had considered working with, Dr. Reverdy, was also leaving to take up a full time role with Amazon in Seattle. I tried to convince him to take me on anyway, since he was keeping his appointment to advise his current students, but that didn't work out.

Rejection 2 (or 4? 5?)

Fast forward to this semester (fall 2020), and I started up a conversation with Dr. Thanga. We emailed back and forth and had a few zoom calls over the course of a few weeks, but ultimately he also dropped me like a hot potato once he learned I was self funded, opinionated, and not going to be easily coerced into whatever bitch work he had lying around.

So, here we are. It's my birthday (20201118), I'm nearly finished with what should be my last semester of classes ever, and I'm still without an advisor or even a hint of a plan for my research and dissertation. Woo.
